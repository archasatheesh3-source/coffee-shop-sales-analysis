 Project Overview

This project focuses on analysing coffee shop sales data to uncover key business insights and trends.
The workflow includes Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Google Colab and building an interactive dashboard using Power BI to visualise customer behaviour, product performance, and revenue patterns.

The primary goal is to help the coffee shop understand sales trends, peak hours, popular products, and payment preferences to support better business decisions.

 Key Insights

A few major findings from the analysis:

Peak sales occur during morning and early evening hours.

Certain coffee items consistently generate higher revenue.

Card payments dominate over cash transactions.

Seasonal and weekday trends show strong influence on customer footfall.
